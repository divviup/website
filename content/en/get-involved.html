There are many opportunities to get engaged while waiting for Divvi Up's launch, such as:

Sign up for the waitlist

Interested in trying out Divvi Up when it becomes available? Sign up to be added to our waitlist.

Get Updates from ISRG and Divvi Up

You can subscribe to the Divvi Up newsletter using the form at the bottom of this page.

Participate in the IETF Discussion List

We are working in an open and transparent manner to develop a standardized version of the protocol that underpins Divvi Up. The Internet Engineering Task Force (IETF) is a longstanding standards body that is hosting the development of this standard through a working group called PPM.

  • Sign up for the PPM Mailing List
  • Learn more about PPM and the IETF standardization process

Contribute

Visit our projects on Github and contribute to the project.

See our Open Source Repositories

Sponsor

Development of Divvi Up is made possible thanks to a diverse group of funders. If you or your company is interested in helping fund continued development of Divvi Up, email us.
