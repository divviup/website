Every component of Divvi Up is open source and available on GitHub. Further, the core protocol, Distributed Aggregation Protocol (DAP), is on a path to become a Internet Engineering Task Force (IETF) standard co-developed by Internet Security Research Group (ISRG, best known known for Let's Encrypt), Cloudflare, and Mozilla.
